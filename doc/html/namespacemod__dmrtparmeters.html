Compute radiative transfer parameters (extinction and single scattering albedo) using DMRT theory under the assumption: QCA-CP, small particules, sticky short range, single species.

Warning: this only works for short range, i.e. moderate stickiness / small grains (high tau value)!!

Parameters:
[in]frequencyfrequency in Hertz
[in]temperaturetemperature in Kelvin
[in]ffractional volume
[in]aradius in meter
[in]taustickiness
[in]wetnessfractional volume of water with respect to ice
[in]mediumtype of medium (valid value is S=Snow or I=ice with air bubbles)
[out]Eeff0effective dielectric constant, order 0 (=no scattering)
[out]Eeffeffective dielectric constant
[out]albedosingle scattering albedo
[out]betaextinction coefficient (also known as Ke)
[in]Eiceoptional dielectric constant of ice (don't use it)
